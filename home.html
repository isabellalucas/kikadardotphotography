About Me

My name is Kika and I’m a Vancouver photographer living in beautiful British Columbia. I specialize mainly in headshots, portraits and events. I also love capturing lifestyle sessions, X, and Y. I was first introduced to photography during my high school years. The school offered me film and I would shoot rolls and rolls since the school paid to develop it. Photography quickly turned into an expensive hobby and to fund that hobby I started shooting weddings with other established photographers, where I quickly learned how to photograph people. I’m happy to say that hobby has now turned into something I’m very passionate about. I’ve always been a creative individual. Though I’m not very good with a paintbrush or a pencil, I can tell a story through the images I create. I love interacting with individuals, couples, and kids, capturing their emotions. Trying to get them to be themselves in front of the camera can be hard and challenging but it’s what makes taking photos that much more rewarding for me. If you have a project in mind, would like your headshot or portrait taken, need a special event, be it a wedding, corporate event, or something personal captured, feel free to get in touch! I’d love to hear from you.
